About Us

Welcome to Safeguard Orphanage, a place of hope, care, and compassion for orphaned children. Established in 2022 Safeguard Orphanage is dedicated to providing a loving and nurturing environment for children who have lost their parents, ensuring they grow up with the support and resources they need to thrive.we are committed to providing the love, education, and guidance necessary to make that happen.
